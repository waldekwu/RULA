RULA - Rapid Upper Limb Assessment

Osmond Group Limited (Osmond) have taken every care in preparing this resource, it must be used according to the guidelines based on the original article* by Prof E.N. Corlett and Dr L. McAtamney. No responsibility will be taken by Osmond in the use of this software. RULA provides a score of a snapshot of the activity as part of a rapid screening tool. For further information on methodology refer to our on-line guidance or: McAtamney, L and Corlett, E.N. Reducing the risks of work related upper limb disorders - A guide and methods.
